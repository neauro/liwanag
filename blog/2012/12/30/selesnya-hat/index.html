Selesnya Hat

Dec 30th, 2012

with bonus ears

Hat! Fleece! Felt! The tree came out a little lopsided but I think it was good for a first try. I intended for it to have more of a chullo-shape but in the end confused myself and ended up just going back to the familiar rectangular shape the yields triangle ears.

I have a lot more brown and white fleece left which I hope to use for future corgi- or shiba-related endeavors!
