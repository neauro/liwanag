What's the difference between Number and int?
The ranges --

Number goes from 4.9406564584124654e-324 to 1.79769313486231e+308
int goes from -2147483648 to 2147483647 (whole numbers only)
uint goes from 0 to 4294967295 (whole numbers only)

Additionally, Number's default type is NaN (Not a Number), and can't be undefined or null. Number is kind of like float and double, I suppose.
