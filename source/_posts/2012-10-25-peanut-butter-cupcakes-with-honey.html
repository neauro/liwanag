I finally had the chance to make cupcakes the other day for the Halloween Sakura-Con staff party!

yum

These are peanut butter and jelly cupcakes from the Martha Stewart cupcake book, which I appreciate for its large, inspiring photos and general ability to strike me with cupcake inspiration every once in a while. Unlike other cupcake books, it contains a variety of recipes, and not just different ways to arrange colored frost for the holidays.

For these cupcakes, I lacked jelly, so instead used some "huckleberry creamed" honey retrieved from a market near Ellensberg.

i think they still tasted fine

I'm not actually a fan of peanut butter sweets, so next time I'll probably go for the tried-and-true (and favorite) Oreo cheesecake cupcakes, or the blackberry cornmeal ones.
