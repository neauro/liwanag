This creature's jaw is falling off and yet her boobs are smooth as a newborn.  It's an unnecessary (and hilariously disturbing) sexualization.  It's not even relevant to her flavor text, which is something bland about how those with resentments in life are evidently doomed to become (very well-endowed) unhallowed in their afterlife.

Contrast with Diregraf Ghoul, who appears to be a monstrous woman, but doesn't indulge the male gaze:

(source)

I don't want every monstrous character to be "ugly."  But cards featuring male-looking creatures have so much variety: Abattoir Ghoul, Grimgrin, Elder of Laurels, Disciple of Griselbrand.  In contrast, female-looking characters in the majority are pale-skinned, slender, and/or have large boobs.
