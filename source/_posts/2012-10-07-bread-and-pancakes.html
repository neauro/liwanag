On the recommendation of my friend, I checked out Artisan Bread in 5 Minutes a Day from the library and attempted its secrets.

Having never made bread before and heard much of the difficulty of doing so, I was pretty skeptical. In the end it did not, in fact, take me five minutes, but forty or so minutes (from "cloaking" the bread to throwing it in the oven to eating it with delight) is not that bad.

My first attempt was remarkably crusty and white and fluffy inside.

WHOA. did i really do that?

My second attempt was crusted with soot as black as midnight and filled the entire house with smoke besides. But, the inside was still white and fluffy!

and edible!

Another first for me was creating pancakes from a macadamia-nut studded mix. I have to say that I kind of adore the speckled pattern that craters across pancake bottoms, all golden and moony.
