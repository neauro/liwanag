screenshot of bookmarklet "save" view

I received Delicious' bookmarklet layout change today and it's a lot prettier — I wish I could compare it to the previous one — but for whatever reason I couldn't immediately tell where to input my tags. The light lines, the title beneath the input — it's weird — it's like the hospital or government forms that I always take two of whenever I need to fill one out, because I always put my address and phone number in the wrong box.

This is the sort of impediment you feel only at first glance, maybe. I hitched for a couple minutes but now I get it. However, with the previous bookmarklet, I liked that "tags" was a huge box, and I could click anywhere within it to start input. Now I have to click either the invisible input box, or the light "tags" title. (Though I notice now that when you hover over tags, it does darken the input line somewhat.)

screenshot of bookmarklet "home" view

Another improvement to the bookmarklet: before, after saving a link to Delicious, the pop-up turned completely white and there was no indication that your link was saved successfully. This time, you see a small version of your Delicious homepage, with your newly-saved link at the top. This still doesn't give you feedback if you open the bookmarklet on an already-saved page and alter tags or comments, however.
