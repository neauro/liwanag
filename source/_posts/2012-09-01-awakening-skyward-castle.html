I never thought I'd actually go so far as to purchase a hidden object game instead of just playing the demos, but the Awakening series is so fun. I like that it has a focus mainly on puzzles (all of which always seem unique and new), and besides that the scenes are just beautiful.

you can't even see the particle effects floatin' around this thing, wuuuut

Like, don't you just want to be here?

c'mon! who could turn down a flying waterfall tree?

The voice acting is a little weird, but overall Awakening is a good source of dopamine yielded by doing A to get B which is needed for C to get D for E, etc.
