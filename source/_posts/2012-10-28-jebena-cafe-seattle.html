just when you think you've tasted everything

This is ful medames, served for breakfast at Jebena Cafe, an Ethiopian restaurant Andou and I discovered some time ago. It contains fava beans, olive oil, tomatoes, green onions, feta, boiled egg, and other things I can't remember. You eat it with crisp, freshly-made baguette bread, and it's one of the most startlingly wonderful and fresh tastes I've ever discovered.
